Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 13 May 2026

We understand life happens. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to you, fair to other clients on the waitlist, and respectful of the time Luana sets aside specifically for your appointment.

1. The short version

  • 48 hours' notice or more: Free reschedule or cancellation. Deposit fully refunded or transferred to your new booking.
  • Less than 48 hours' notice: Deposit forfeited. A new deposit will be required to rebook.
  • No-shows: Full deposit forfeited. We may require full prepayment for any future bookings.

2. How to reschedule or cancel

The fastest way is to reply to your confirmation email or message Luana directly on WhatsApp at +61 450 349 025. You can also email aestheticsbylua@gmail.com. We'll move things around as quickly as we can.

3. Late arrivals

We hold appointments for up to 10 minutes after the scheduled start time. After that, your treatment may be shortened to respect the next client's booking — the full treatment price still applies. Please send a quick message if you're running late so we can plan accordingly.

4. Studio cancellations

In the rare case we need to cancel or reschedule your appointment (illness, emergency, studio circumstance), you'll be offered the soonest available reschedule or a full refund of your deposit, whichever you prefer.

5. Group bookings & special occasions

For multi-treatment bookings, bridal parties or group sessions, a larger deposit may be required and 7 days' notice is preferred for any changes. We'll communicate this clearly at the time of booking.

6. Why this policy exists

As a boutique single-therapist studio, every appointment represents time that Luana has set aside personally for you. Late cancellations and no-shows mean another client could not have your spot, and the time cannot be recovered. Thank you for understanding — and for respecting Luana's time.

7. Exceptional circumstances

Genuine emergencies and unexpected illness are handled with care. Please get in touch and we'll work something out — we are reasonable humans.

8. Refund processing

Refunds (when applicable) are processed via the original payment method within 5–10 business days. Stripe processing times vary slightly between banks.

9. Australian Consumer Law

Nothing in this policy limits your statutory rights under the Australian Consumer Law.

Questions?

Email aestheticsbylua@gmail.com or message Luana on WhatsApp.
