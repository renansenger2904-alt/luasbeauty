Meet The Founder

A studio
built on care.

Lua's Beauty was founded by Luana — a Brazilian beauty professional whose work is rooted in considered care, calm hands, and the belief that every treatment should be deeply personal.

01 / 03 The Story

01 / The Story

From Brazil to North Perth.

Every treatment at Lua's is personalised. The goal is not to change your face — it's to enhance your natural beauty, support your skin confidence, and create a quiet hour of care that feels considered from the moment you arrive.

Luana trained for six years at Ale Palhano Beauty in Brazil, where she developed her detail-driven approach to skin care, lash and brow work, and the sensorial side of beauty treatment.

02 / The Studio

A boutique studio, one at a time.

After moving to Perth, Luana opened Lua's Beauty as a quiet space dedicated to one client at a time — boutique, unhurried, deeply personal.

Inside Avani Hair on Fitzgerald Street, the studio is designed to feel less like a salon and more like a quiet escape. The kind of room that calms your nervous system the moment you arrive.

03 / The Approach

Unhurried, considered, yours.

Treatments are planned around your skin, your sensitivities, and the rhythm of your week — never a template, never an upsell.

You leave with a clear plan, genuine aftercare guidance, and skin that feels well looked after. The kind of subtle, lasting result that only comes from real craft.

The Philosophy

Three things we hold quietly.

How we care, how we communicate, and how every client feels when they leave — these are the things we measure ourselves on.

01

Skin health, first.

We don't chase quick fixes or trends. Every treatment is designed to support healthier-looking skin over time — through hydration, considered exfoliation, barrier care, and genuine product knowledge.

02

Natural enhancement.

The goal is never to make you look like someone else. Brows should still look like yours — softer, fuller. Skin should feel like yours, on its best day. Subtlety is the standard.

03

A calm experience.

Beauty appointments should feel restorative. Soft music, warm linen, an unhurried conversation. You'll never feel rushed, sold to, or treated like a number on a calendar.
