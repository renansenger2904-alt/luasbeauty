What Is Bioage

Brazilian cosmeceuticals,
at clinic grade.

Bioage is one of Brazil's most respected professional skincare lines — formulated specifically for in-clinic use, with concentrations of actives that are not available in retail products. Lua's Beauty was founded by Luana — a Brazilian beauty professional with six years of experience working with these protocols — and the studio is among very few in Perth offering the complete range. Each protocol is carefully selected to address a specific concern, and most see best results as a series of 4 to 6 sessions.

— Why these work

Targeted, active, results-focused.

Unlike a general facial, a Bioage protocol is built around a specific concern — and combines the right active ingredients, the right concentration and the right delivery technique to address that concern visibly. It's the difference between maintenance and meaningful change.

The trade-off is commitment: most protocols deliver their best results as a series of 4 to 6 sessions, spaced 1 to 2 weeks apart. The investment is real — but for clients who've struggled with pigmentation, congestion, or ageing concerns for years, the results often are too.

Common Questions

Things worth knowing.

Honest answers to the questions our North Perth clients ask most.

How is a Bioage protocol different from a regular facial?
A regular facial is generalist — cleanse, exfoliate, hydrate. A protocol is specialist — built around a specific concern, with clinical-grade actives at concentrations only available in professional treatments.
Do I really need a series? Can't I just do one?
You can absolutely do one — and it will feel wonderful. But for visible change in pigmentation, fine lines or congestion, the actives need consistent exposure over weeks. One session is maintenance; a series is transformation.
Is Bioage available in Australia?
Bioage is a Brazilian professional brand and is not widely distributed in Australia. Lua's Beauty is among very few studios in Perth offering the full clinical range, brought to North Perth by Luana from Brazil.
Will my skin be red afterwards?
Most protocols have minimal downtime — light flushing for 1–2 hours is the most common reaction. We always advise on aftercare specific to your protocol.
How do I know which protocol to start with?
Book a complimentary 30-minute skin consultation. Luana will analyse your skin, listen to your goals, and recommend the right starting point — with no obligation.
