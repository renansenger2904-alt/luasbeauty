What It Is

Brows that look
fuller, naturally.

Brow Lamination is the gentlest way to transform sparse, unruly or asymmetrical brows into a beautifully sculpted, fuller-looking shape. The treatment carefully lifts and sets each brow hair into its most flattering position — adding visible volume, evening out gaps, and creating the kind of soft, brushed-up brow that's defined feeds for the past three years. Without microblading, without tattoos, without permanent commitment.

— Why lamination, not microblading

Refined, never permanent.

Microblading and brow tattoos create permanent or semi-permanent change. Lamination doesn't. It uses a gentle lifting solution to reshape your existing hairs into a fuller, more defined arch — and the effect lasts 6 to 8 weeks before naturally softening as new hairs grow in.

It's the perfect treatment for women who want fuller-looking brows without committing to anything permanent — and for those whose brow style has evolved with the trends.

The Treatment

How your brows are shaped.

Every Brow Lamination at Lua's begins with a thorough consultation — we never treat your brows without first agreeing on the exact shape you want.

01

Shape consultation

We discuss your natural shape, the look you're after, and the level of lift that will suit your features.

02

Cleanse & prep

Brows are cleansed, dried and brushed into their starting position before the treatment begins.

03

Lifting solution

A gentle solution is applied and timed precisely — softening the hairs so they can be carefully reshaped.

04

Set & finish

Hairs are brushed into their new position, set with a second solution, then nourished, tinted (if added) and shaped.

Common Questions

Things worth knowing.

Honest answers to the questions our North Perth clients ask most.

How long does Brow Lamination last?
6 to 8 weeks. Your brows will look most defined for the first 4 weeks, then gradually soften as new hairs grow in.
Will it damage my brow hairs?
Not when done properly with quality solutions and the right timing — we always finish with a nourishing serum to support hair health.
Should I add the tint?
If your brows are naturally light or sparse, the tint adds significantly more visible definition. Most clients add it. Brow Lamination + Tint is also available — ask Luana when booking.
Can I wash my brows after?
We recommend keeping them dry and product-free for the first 24 hours, then resuming your normal routine.
Can lamination fix sparse brows?
Lamination creates the illusion of fuller brows by lifting existing hairs to cover gaps. It works beautifully on most sparseness, though severe gaps may benefit from also exploring brow growth serums.
