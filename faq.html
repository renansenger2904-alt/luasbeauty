Helpful Information

Frequently asked questions.

Everything you might want to know — about treatments, bookings, payment, preparation and aftercare. Not finding what you need? Send us a message.

Booking & Appointments

How do I book a treatment?
Book online via the booking page — choose your treatment, date, time and pay a small deposit. You can also message Luana on WhatsApp at +61 450 349 025 or email aestheticsbylua@gmail.com.
What if I'm not sure which treatment is right for me?
Start with a complimentary 30-minute skin consultation. Luana will assess your skin, listen to your concerns and recommend a personalised plan. No payment is required for the consultation.
Do I need to pay a deposit?
For paid treatments, a small deposit (typically AUD $30–$50, or around 20–30% of the treatment price) secures your appointment. The deposit is deducted from your final balance. The complimentary consultation requires no payment.
What is your cancellation policy?
We ask for at least 48 hours' notice to reschedule or cancel. Late cancellations (within 48 hours) and no-shows may forfeit the deposit. See the full Cancellation Policy.
Can I reschedule online?
Yes. Use the link in your confirmation email, or reply directly — we'll move your booking to a new time at no charge, as long as you give 48 hours' notice.

Treatments

How long does a treatment take?
Most facials run 60–90 minutes. Lash lifts take around 60 minutes; brow lamination around 45 minutes. The complimentary consultation is 30 minutes. Each booking includes a few minutes of arrival and aftercare conversation.
What's the difference between dermaplaning and microdermabrasion?
Dermaplaning is a manual exfoliation that gently removes dead skin and facial fuzz, leaving skin smooth and bright. Microdermabrasion uses a fine, crystal-free abrasion to refresh the skin surface — designed to support hydration and a healthier-looking glow. Luana will recommend whichever suits your skin best.
Is LED therapy safe?
LED therapy is a non-invasive light treatment generally well tolerated. Red light is designed to support the appearance of collagen and elastin. There are some contraindications — including certain medications and conditions — which Luana will discuss with you during consultation.
How often should I come for facials?
For most people, every 4–6 weeks is a good rhythm. Specific concerns (pigmentation, congestion, ageing) may benefit from a more frequent series. Luana will recommend a personalised cadence.
Are treatments safe during pregnancy?
Many treatments can be safely modified for pregnancy, while some are not recommended. Please let us know in advance — we'll adjust your treatment plan and skip any contraindicated products or modalities.

Preparation & Aftercare

How should I prepare for my facial?
Avoid retinol, AHAs and other actives for 3–5 days before your facial. Don't exfoliate, wax, or have laser treatments in the 48 hours prior. Come with clean skin if possible — you can also remove makeup at the studio.
What should I do after a facial?
Avoid heavy exercise, saunas, and direct sun for 24 hours. Skip retinol and strong actives for a few days. Drink water, wear SPF, and use gentle, hydrating products.
How long does a lash lift last?
A lash lift typically lasts 6–8 weeks, depending on your natural lash cycle. Avoid water and steam for the first 24 hours.
How long does brow lamination last?
Brow lamination typically lasts 4–6 weeks. Avoid water, makeup and oils in the first 24 hours, and brush your brows daily afterwards for a polished look.

Are deposits refundable?
