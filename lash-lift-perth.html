What It Is

Your own lashes,
beautifully awakened.

A Lash Lift is the gentlest way to transform your eyes — without extensions, without daily upkeep, without the cost or commitment of regular fills. The treatment lifts your natural lashes from the root, creating a curl that opens up the eye, makes lashes appear visibly longer, and leaves you looking refreshed for 6 to 8 weeks. The most natural way to wake up looking awake.

— Why a lift, not extensions

The quieter alternative.

Lash extensions are beautiful, but they require glue, fills every 2–3 weeks, and careful aftercare to keep your natural lashes healthy. A lash lift requires none of that. There are no glues, no synthetics, and no maintenance — just a gentle reshaping of your own lashes that lets them stand at their most flattering angle.

The result is more subtle and infinitely more low-maintenance than extensions — perfect for women who want to look polished without looking 'done'.

The Treatment

One hour, every detail considered.

Lash lifts at Lua's are performed slowly and precisely — every lash placed individually for the most beautiful, even curl.

01

Consultation

We discuss the level of lift you want — natural, medium or maximum — and the curl shape that suits your eye shape best.

02

Lash placement

Each lash is gently placed on a silicone shield matched to your lash length — slow, precise, individual.

03

Lifting solution

Two carefully timed solutions reshape and set the lashes into their new lifted position.

04

Nourish & finish

A nourishing serum is applied to support lash health — and you sit up to a quietly transformed gaze.

Common Questions

Things worth knowing.

Honest answers to the questions our North Perth clients ask most.

How long does a lash lift last?
6 to 8 weeks — the full cycle of your natural lash regrowth. You'll start to notice it gradually softening as new lashes grow in.
Will it damage my lashes?
Not when performed correctly with quality solutions. Our lifting formulations are gentle, and we always finish with a nourishing serum.
Should I get the tint as well?
Most clients do. The Lash Lift + Tint ($120) gives you visibly darker, defined lashes that eliminate the need for mascara entirely.
How soon can I get my lashes wet?
We recommend keeping lashes dry for 24 hours after the treatment. After that, treat them entirely as normal.
Can I have a lift if I wear contacts?
Yes — just remove your contacts before the appointment and wait until after to put them back in.
