Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 May 2026

Lua's Beauty ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store and disclose personal information that we obtain through our website, online booking system, in-studio consultations and any other interactions you have with us. We comply with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles.

1. The information we collect

We may collect the following information when you book a treatment, contact us, or use our website:

  • Identity details — name, email address, phone number, date of birth (if relevant to a treatment).
  • Booking details — treatment selected, date and time, special requests, skin concerns and history.
  • Health-related information you voluntarily share — allergies, medications, pregnancy status, contraindications relevant to treatment safety.
  • Payment information — processed securely by Stripe; we do not store full card details on our systems.
  • Technical information — IP address, browser type, device details, and pages viewed on our website.

2. How we use your information

  • To process your booking, prepare your treatment plan and provide aftercare.
  • To communicate with you regarding appointments, reminders and follow-ups.
  • To process payments and refunds via our payment partner Stripe.
  • To improve our services, treatment offerings and website experience.
  • To send occasional updates, promotions and skin care education — only with your consent. You can opt out at any time.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

3. How we store and protect your information

We use industry-standard security measures, including encrypted databases, secure HTTPS transmission, and limited staff access. Payment information is processed by Stripe (a PCI-DSS compliant provider) and is not stored on our servers. Despite reasonable precautions, no system is 100% secure — we cannot guarantee absolute protection against unauthorised access.

4. Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only with:

  • Trusted service providers (booking software, email/SMS provider, payment processor) under appropriate confidentiality obligations.
  • Government bodies or law enforcement where legally required.
  • With your explicit consent — for example, if you authorise us to share before-and-after images.

5. Before-and-after photos & testimonials

We only publish images, results or testimonials with your written consent. You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us, and we will remove the content from public-facing channels as promptly as reasonably practicable.

6. Cookies & analytics

Our website uses cookies to support functionality and basic analytics (such as understanding which pages are most visited). You can disable cookies in your browser, though this may affect certain features.

7. Your rights

  • You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • You can request correction or deletion of your data at any time.
  • You can opt out of marketing communications via the unsubscribe link in any email, or by contacting us directly.
  • You can make a complaint to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) if you believe we have mishandled your information.

8. Contact us

For any privacy enquiries, please email aestheticsbylua@gmail.com or write to us at Inside Avani Hair, Level 2 / 331 Fitzgerald Street, North Perth WA 6006.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most recent version is always available on this page, with the last-updated date displayed at the top.
