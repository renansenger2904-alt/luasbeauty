What It Is

An instant glow,
visibly plumper skin.

The Hydra Oxygen Facial is the studio's signature hydration treatment — combining gentle resurfacing with oxygen and serum infusion to flood the skin with moisture, lift away dullness and reveal an immediate, glassy finish. It's the facial our North Perth clients book before weddings, holidays, milestone events — and increasingly, just because.

— How it works

Three stages of renewal.

The treatment combines three carefully sequenced stages: gentle resurfacing to lift away dead cells and surface congestion, followed by deep hydration with hyaluronic and antioxidant serums, and finished with oxygen infusion that drives the actives deeper while plumping and brightening the complexion.

The result is skin that looks visibly hydrated, lit-from-within and glassy — without irritation, without redness, without downtime.

Common Questions

Things worth knowing.

Honest answers to the questions our North Perth clients ask most.

How long does the Hydra Oxygen glow last?
The immediate glow lasts up to 7–10 days. With a series of 4 treatments spaced 2–4 weeks apart, the hydration and brightening benefits become cumulative and longer-lasting.
Is this the same as a HydraFacial?
It's a similar concept — gentle resurfacing combined with serum infusion. We use professional Brazilian technology and Bioage cosmeceuticals selected by Luana for their results-focused formulations.
How soon before an event should I book?
Anywhere from the day before to 3 days before is ideal. There's no risk of post-treatment redness, so booking on the day of your event is also possible.
Can I combine it with other treatments?
Absolutely. Many clients pair the Hydra Oxygen Facial with a Lash Lift or Brow Lamination on the same day for a complete pre-event refresh.
