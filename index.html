Meet the Founder

From Brazil to North Perth, with care.

Lua's Beauty was founded with love and passion by Luana — a dedicated beauty professional originally from Brazil.

With six years of experience at the renowned Ale Palhano Beauty in Brazil, Luana has spent her career helping clients feel their absolute best. After moving to Perth, she turned her dream into reality — opening Lua's Beauty as a space where treatments go beyond aesthetics, focusing instead on confidence, self-care, and the quiet kind of beauty that comes from genuinely well-cared-for skin.

Her approach is detail-driven and deeply personal. Treatments are tailored, conversations are unhurried, and every client leaves feeling radiant, refreshed and a little more themselves.
