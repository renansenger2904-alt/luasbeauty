Common Questions

Things worth knowing.

Honest answers to the questions our North Perth clients ask most.

How often should I have a facial?
For most skin types, every 4 to 6 weeks is ideal — that's the cycle of natural cell renewal. If you're addressing a specific concern with a Bioage protocol, we'll often recommend a series of 4 to 6 weekly or fortnightly sessions to begin.
Which facial is right for me if I've never had one?
We'd usually recommend starting with a complimentary 30-minute skin consultation, or The Glow Facial ($130) — a beautifully balanced introduction to skin care at Lua's.
Will my skin react after the treatment?
Our facials are designed for zero or minimal downtime. Some skin types may experience light flushing for 1–2 hours post-treatment, particularly after Dermaplaning or Bioage protocols — this settles quickly.
How do I book a facial in North Perth?
The fastest way is to message Luana directly on WhatsApp at +61 450 349 025 — replies are typically within the hour. You can also send an email enquiry.
Where is the studio located?
Lua's Beauty is in North Perth, Western Australia, operating by appointment only. Full address details are shared upon booking confirmation.
