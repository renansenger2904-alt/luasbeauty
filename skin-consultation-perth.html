Why Begin Here

The most considered way
to start.

Most beauty studios will book you straight into a treatment — sometimes the right one, sometimes not. At Lua's, we'd rather take 30 minutes to get to know your skin first. The consultation is free, completely confidential, and never includes a pitch. You leave with a clear understanding of your skin, a personalised recommendation, and absolutely no pressure to book a follow-up. Many clients take the plan home, think it over, and come back when ready. That's exactly how it should be.

— What we cover

Your skin, properly understood.

We start with a careful analysis under examination light — the kind of close, professional look at your skin that you can't do at home with a mirror. Then we talk: about your routine, your goals, your concerns, and what you've tried before. By the end, you'll know more about your own skin than you probably ever have.

Then we build the plan. It might be one of our signature facials. It might be a Bioage protocol series. It might just be a few changes to your home routine. Whatever's right for you — that's what we'll recommend, with no expectation that you book anything.

Common Questions

Things worth knowing.

Honest answers to the questions our North Perth clients ask most.

Is the consultation really free?
Yes — completely. There's no obligation to book any treatment afterwards, and we never use the consultation as a sales pitch. Many of our regulars first came in for a consultation that they didn't book on the day.
How is this different from a treatment?
There's no treatment performed during the consultation — it's purely an analysis and conversation. If you'd like a treatment afterwards, that's a separate appointment.
What should I bring?
Just yourself, ideally with no makeup so we can see your skin clearly. If you'd like, bring your current skincare products so we can review them together.
How long does the consultation take?
30 minutes — a careful, unhurried conversation, but we respect your time and won't run over.
How do I book a free consultation?
Message Luana directly on WhatsApp at +61 450 349 025 — typically the fastest path. Or send an enquiry through our contact page.
